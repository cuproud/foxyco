# GigPilot – Free AI Assistant for Gig Drivers (Project Master Plan)

## Vision

GigPilot is a completely free, Android-native application designed to become the ultimate companion for gig economy drivers. Unlike existing apps that require monthly subscriptions or have limited regional support, GigPilot aims to provide powerful automation, analytics, profit optimization, and business management tools at no cost while prioritizing user privacy.

The app is inspired by the best features from Maxymo, Mystro, GigU, DriverPal, and Gridwise, but it is designed from the ground up with a modern architecture, offline-first capabilities, and modular development.

---

# Objectives

The application should help drivers:

* Earn more money
* Drive fewer unnecessary kilometers
* Reduce idle time
* Make smarter order decisions
* Track every dollar earned
* Track every business expense
* Automatically calculate profit
* Prepare taxes more easily
* Improve long-term earnings through analytics
* Maintain complete ownership of their data

# Development Philosophy

The project should be developed in clearly defined phases.

Each module should:

* Be independently testable
* Follow Clean Architecture
* Be reusable
* Have comprehensive documentation
* Support future expansion
* Minimize technical debt
* Remain highly maintainable

Every feature should prioritize performance, battery efficiency, accessibility, privacy, and a polished user experience.

GigPilot is intended to become a long-term open, extensible platform that empowers gig drivers with professional-grade tools without requiring paid subscriptions.
